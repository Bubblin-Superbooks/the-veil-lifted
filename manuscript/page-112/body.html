The chemicals were ours, and the development took place in the presence of my second eldest daughter, in the dark room before referred to, and to which we all had access during the whole process. I therefore claim that this demonstration has been a complete success, because every test condition has been complied with. As ‘Salem Scudder’ puts it, in a certain sensational scene in Dion Boucicault’s well-known drama of the Octoroon, ‘I guess the apparatus can’t lie.’ The apparatus, i.e., the camera, has certainly not lied to us. I have also to state that our family medium saw our son in the room ‘posed’ in front of the camera during the sitting and pointed out the place where he stood, before the medium put the slide into the instrument.
